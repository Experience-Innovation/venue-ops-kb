# AV & Production

Audio, video, lighting, rigging, production management, and broadcast infrastructure within venues. This domain covers both permanent house systems and the technical production capabilities that support events ranging from corporate presentations to live concerts and broadcast productions.

## Scope

### In Scope

Audio systems (PA, distributed, in-ear monitoring), video systems (LED walls, projection, IMAG), lighting systems (house, stage, architectural), rigging and fly systems, production management and stage management, broadcast and streaming infrastructure, production power distribution, technical labor management, production equipment inventory.

### Out of Scope

IT networking and Wi-Fi (covered in technology-and-digital), event coordination and show calling (covered in event-operations), AV equipment procurement (covered in supply-chain-and-procurement).

## Key Functions

House AV system operation and maintenance, production management, stage management, rigging operations, broadcast and streaming, lighting design and operation, audio engineering, video engineering, production power management, technical labor coordination.
