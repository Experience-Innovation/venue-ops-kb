# Booking & Sales

Event booking, client services, contracting, and CRM for venues that host third-party events. This domain covers the sales cycle from initial inquiry through contract execution and the ongoing client relationship management that drives event bookings.

## Scope

### In Scope

Event inquiry and lead management, venue tours and site inspections, proposal development, contract negotiation and execution, client relationship management (CRM), event specification guides, booking calendar management, rate card and pricing structures, rebooking and retention strategies.

### Out of Scope

Event day execution (covered in event-operations), ticket sales for booked events (covered in ticketing-and-box-office), F&B catering proposals (covered in food-and-beverage).

## Key Functions

Sales and lead generation, venue tours and client hosting, proposal and contract management, CRM system management, event specification development, calendar and availability management, rate optimization, client retention and rebooking.
