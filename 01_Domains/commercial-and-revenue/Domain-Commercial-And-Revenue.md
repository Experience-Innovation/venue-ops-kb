# Commercial & Revenue

Revenue generation through naming rights, sponsorship, premium sales, yield management, and commercial partnerships. This domain covers the strategies and operations that maximize venue revenue beyond core ticket sales and event hosting fees.

## Scope

### In Scope

Naming rights agreements, sponsorship sales and activation, yield management and dynamic pricing, commercial partnership development, ancillary revenue programs, merchandise and retail, revenue diversification strategies, commercial use of venue assets, non-event-day revenue.

### Out of Scope

Ticket pricing and sales operations (covered in ticketing-and-box-office), premium hospitality delivery (covered in premium-and-vip), marketing campaigns (covered in marketing-and-communications).

## Key Functions

Sponsorship sales and relationship management, naming rights negotiation, yield management strategy, commercial partnership development, ancillary revenue program management, merchandise operations, revenue analytics and forecasting.
