# Crowd Management

The science and practice of managing large gatherings safely and efficiently. This domain covers crowd dynamics, flow analysis, density monitoring, ingress and egress planning, queue management, and the predictive tools that help venues move people safely through spaces.

## Scope

### In Scope

Crowd dynamics and behavior, flow analysis and modeling, density monitoring and thresholds, ingress and egress planning, queue management, pedestrian modeling, crowd safety barriers and infrastructure, event-day crowd monitoring, festival and standing-event management, crush prevention.

### Out of Scope

Physical security response (covered in security), emergency evacuation (covered in safety-and-risk), parking and transit flow (covered in parking-and-transportation).

## Key Functions

Crowd flow modeling and simulation, density monitoring operations, ingress and egress management, queue design and management, crowd safety infrastructure deployment, real-time crowd monitoring, post-event crowd analysis, crowd management training.
