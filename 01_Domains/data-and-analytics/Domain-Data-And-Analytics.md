# Data & Analytics

Business intelligence, reporting, dashboards, data architecture, and predictive analytics that inform venue operational decisions. This domain covers how venues collect, store, analyze, and act on data across all operational functions.

## Scope

### In Scope

Business intelligence and reporting, operational dashboards, data architecture and warehousing, predictive analytics and modeling, customer data platforms, survey and feedback analytics, financial and operational KPIs, data governance and quality, performance benchmarking, data integration across systems.

### Out of Scope

IT infrastructure for data systems (covered in technology-and-digital), specific operational metrics (tracked within each domain), AI applications (covered across relevant domains).

## Key Functions

BI platform management, dashboard development, data architecture design, predictive model development, KPI definition and tracking, data quality management, cross-system data integration, performance reporting, benchmarking analysis.
