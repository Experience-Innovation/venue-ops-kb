# Retractable Roof Operations and Weather Decision Protocols

Retractable roof venues (Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Globe Life Field, Rogers Centre, SoFi Stadium, Chase Field) require operational protocols that do not exist in fixed-roof facilities — weather decision authority chains, mechanical operation procedures, and HVAC transition management.

## Weather Decision Authority

Under NFL policy, the home team must notify the referee whether the roof will be open or closed no later than 90 minutes before kickoff; the roof must reach its designated position before team warmups. Once the game starts, an open roof may be closed up to the 5:00 mark of the fourth quarter if rain or hazardous weather threatens; after 5:00 Q4, the roof remains in its current position except for public safety emergencies. For playoff games, Football Operations makes the final decision ([[Source-0457-R23-Specialty-Venues-Pt2]]).

## Temperature and Humidity Thresholds

Published operational thresholds include: Mercedes-Benz Stadium approximately 60-75°F for open roof; Globe Life Field 65-80°F with humidity below 50% (humidity creates slipping hazards from condensation on concourses); Chase Field decisions made series-by-series based on temperature, wind, and humidity; Rogers Centre approximately 14-16°C requiring a multi-day warm period before spring opening. MLB general policy: roof closed for rain threat, sustained winds above 30 mph, temperatures below 65°F for night games, or heat index above 88°F.

## Movable Structure Maintenance

Retractable roof maintenance is a specialty engineering discipline. Rogers Centre's 76 original DC motors (1989) required comprehensive modernization by 2015 — a single motor failure caused the entire system to shut down. The 2015 upgrade replaced all 76 motors with AC/VFD technology enabling independent shutdown of individual motors without stopping complete roof operation. Key maintenance intervals: motor inspection/lubrication, rail/track cleaning, bogie wheel/bearing inspection, weather sealing, and control system firmware updates.

## HVAC Transition Management

Opening a roof on a humid day when the interior is air-conditioned creates condensation risk on structural steel, seating, and concourses. Globe Life Field's early operations discovered humidity-related slipping incidents from condensation when humid outdoor air mixed with conditioned interior air — humidity monitoring is now a roof-opening threshold criterion. SoFi Stadium's passive design approach (ETFE canopy solar shading, Pacific sea breezes) eliminates this problem but constrains comfort management to natural ventilation limits.
