# Facilities & Building Systems

The physical plant and engineered systems that keep venues operational, safe, and comfortable. This domain covers HVAC, mechanical/electrical/plumbing (MEP), building envelope, maintenance programs, asset lifecycle management, capital planning, and space management.

## Scope

### In Scope

HVAC and climate control, MEP systems (mechanical, electrical, plumbing), building envelope and structural maintenance, preventive and predictive maintenance programs, asset lifecycle management, capital planning and replacement cycles, space management and room configuration, cleaning and janitorial services, grounds maintenance, building automation systems (BAS).

### Out of Scope

AV infrastructure (covered in av-and-production), IT network infrastructure (covered in technology-and-digital), sustainability certifications (covered in sustainability-and-environmental).

## Key Functions

Building systems operation and monitoring, preventive maintenance scheduling, capital project management, space allocation and configuration, cleaning operations, grounds and exterior maintenance, energy system management, building automation oversight, asset inventory and lifecycle tracking.
