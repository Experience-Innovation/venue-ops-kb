# Capital Project Delivery and Lifecycle Funding

Venue capital planning encompasses reserve fund methodologies (2-4% of replacement value annually), facility condition assessments, project delivery methods (design-bid-build, design-build, CM-at-Risk, P3), and FF&E lifecycle management.

## Reserve Fund Methodology

Industry benchmarks recommend 2-4% of current replacement value annually. Under-funding creates deferred maintenance backlogs — $1 deferred today costs $3-5 to remediate later.

## Facility Condition Assessment

- **FCI:** Deferred maintenance / current replacement value. Below 5% = good; 5-10% = fair; above 10% = poor
- **CNI:** Projected 10-year capital needs / current replacement value

## Project Delivery Methods

- **Design-Bid-Build:** Traditional sequential — lowest risk, longest timeline
- **Design-Build:** Single entity — faster, fixed price
- **CM-at-Risk:** Guaranteed maximum price during design
- **P3:** Private financing and operation — common for stadiums and arenas
