# Indoor Air Quality and Post-COVID Filtration

Post-pandemic IAQ standards for assembly occupancies now include ASHRAE 241 (infectious aerosol control), enhanced MERV filtration requirements, displacement ventilation strategies, and real-time air quality monitoring integrated with BMS platforms.

## Key Standards

- **ASHRAE 62.1-2022:** Baseline ventilation rates for assembly occupancies
- **ASHRAE 241-2023:** Infection Risk Management Mode — sets equivalent clean air delivery rate targets
- **WELL Building Standard v2:** Air quality credits for enhanced filtration and monitoring

## Filtration Hierarchy

MERV-13 is now the minimum recommended filtration for assembly spaces. Upper-tier venues deploy MERV-14/16 with bipolar ionization or UV-C supplementation.

## Ventilation Strategies

- **Mixed-air systems:** Standard in arenas and stadiums — high volume, moderate control
- **Displacement ventilation:** Preferred for performing arts and lecture halls — delivers fresh air at floor level, exhausts at ceiling
- **Dedicated outdoor air systems (DOAS):** Separates ventilation from thermal conditioning for better humidity control
