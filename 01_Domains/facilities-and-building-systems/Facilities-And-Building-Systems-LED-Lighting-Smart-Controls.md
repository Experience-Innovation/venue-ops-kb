# LED Lighting and Smart Controls

LED retrofit and smart lighting control systems provide 50-70% energy reduction over legacy HID/fluorescent fixtures, with venue-specific requirements for tunable white, RGBW event lighting, daylight harvesting, exterior architectural illumination, and digital signage integration.

## Venue Applications

- **Convention halls:** Tunable white (2700K-6500K) for trade shows, banquets, meetings
- **Arenas/stadiums:** High-output sports lighting with broadcast-grade CRI
- **Exterior:** Architectural LED for building identity and wayfinding
- **Digital signage:** LED ribbon boards and videoboards

## Smart Controls

- **DALI:** Individual fixture control, dimming, color tuning
- **DMX:** Theatrical and event lighting integration
- **Daylight harvesting:** Photosensors reduce artificial light
- **Occupancy-based:** Vacancy sensors in back-of-house areas

## Energy Impact

ASHRAE 90.1-2022 sets max lighting power density at 0.82 W/sq ft for convention/exhibition. LED retrofits achieve 0.4-0.6 W/sq ft.
