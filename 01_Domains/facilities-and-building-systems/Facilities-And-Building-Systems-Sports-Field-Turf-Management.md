# Sports Field and Turf Management

Venue turf management covers both natural grass and artificial turf systems, including mowing, irrigation, aeration for natural fields, and grooming, infill maintenance, and G-max testing for synthetic surfaces, with significant variation by sport and climate.

## Natural Turf

- **Core maintenance:** Mowing, irrigation, fertilization, aeration, overseeding
- **Advanced:** Grow lighting for enclosed stadiums, undersoil heating, drainage management
- **Challenge:** Concert/event damage requiring protective covering systems

## Artificial Turf

- **Core maintenance:** Grooming, infill redistribution, seam inspection, static brush cleaning
- **Safety testing:** G-max impact attenuation per ASTM F355
- **Lifecycle:** 8-10 year replacement cycle; annual infill replenishment of 5-10%
- **Environmental:** Crumb rubber scrutiny driving shift to TPE, cork, and coconut alternatives
