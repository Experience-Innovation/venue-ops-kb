# Venue Cleaning and Environmental Services

Venue cleaning operations encompass event changeover cleaning, day-of-show maintenance, deep cleaning programs, and autonomous cleaning robotics, typically delivered through outsourced or hybrid staffing models with staffing ratios driven by event type and venue size.

## Staffing Models

- **Outsourced:** ABM Industries, Pritchard Industries — most common for large venues
- **Hybrid:** In-house supervisors with contracted cleaning crews
- **Staffing ratios:** Concerts require 2-3x the cleaning staff of trade shows

## Autonomous Cleaning

Robotic floor scrubbers (Avidbots Neo, Tennant T7AMR) are deployed in convention centres for overnight concourse cleaning — 30,000-50,000 sq ft per charge.

## Event Changeover

Clear debris, spot-clean hard surfaces, machine-scrub floors, sanitize restrooms, restock consumables, quality-check before next load-in. Windows can be as tight as 4-6 hours.
