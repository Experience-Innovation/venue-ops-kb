# Financial Operations

Budgeting, profit and loss management, capital planning, insurance, treasury, and the financial governance structures that sustain venue operations. This domain covers the financial backbone that enables investment decisions and operational funding across all other domains.

## Scope

### In Scope

Annual budgeting and forecasting, P&L management by department, capital expenditure planning and approval, insurance programs and risk transfer, treasury and cash management, financial reporting and audit, cost allocation models, contract financial management, tax compliance, financial governance and controls.

### Out of Scope

Revenue generation strategy (covered in commercial-and-revenue), procurement transactions (covered in supply-chain-and-procurement), sponsorship valuation (covered in commercial-and-revenue).

## Key Functions

Budget development and management, financial forecasting, capital planning and prioritization, insurance program management, financial reporting, audit preparation, cost allocation, treasury operations, financial governance, contract financial oversight.
