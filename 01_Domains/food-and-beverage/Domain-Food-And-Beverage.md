# Food & Beverage

All food and beverage operations within venues, representing 30-40% of non-ticket venue revenue. This domain spans kitchen operations, concessions, catering, bars, premium dining, dietary management, food safety compliance, and the technology platforms that support F&B service delivery.

## Scope

### In Scope

Kitchen operations and production, concession stand management, catering services (banquet, plated, buffet), bar and alcohol service, dietary and allergen management, food safety and HACCP compliance, F&B technology (POS, inventory, ordering), menu engineering, commissary operations, mobile and grab-and-go concepts.

### Out of Scope

Supply chain and procurement (covered in supply-chain-and-procurement), liquor licensing (covered in legal-compliance-and-licensing), premium F&B as part of VIP packages (covered in premium-and-vip).

## Key Functions

Kitchen production management, concession operations, catering coordination, alcohol service and compliance, food safety program management, menu development and engineering, F&B technology management, dietary accommodation, waste reduction and sustainability.
