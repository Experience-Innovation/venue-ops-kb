# Guest Experience

The end-to-end journey of every person who attends an event or visits a venue, from pre-arrival through departure and post-event follow-up. This domain encompasses wayfinding, accessibility, comfort, service quality, feedback mechanisms, and the holistic experience that determines whether guests return.

## Scope

### In Scope

Pre-arrival communications and expectations, arrival and ingress experience, wayfinding and signage, seating and sightlines, comfort amenities (restrooms, climate, noise), service interactions, accessibility accommodations, feedback collection and analysis, post-event surveys, Net Promoter Score tracking, guest complaint resolution.

### Out of Scope

F&B service (covered in food-and-beverage), premium/VIP experiences (covered in premium-and-vip), ticketing transactions (covered in ticketing-and-box-office), parking operations (covered in parking-and-transportation).

## Key Functions

Wayfinding and signage design, guest services staffing and training, accessibility program management, feedback and survey systems, service recovery protocols, guest journey mapping, comfort and amenity management.
