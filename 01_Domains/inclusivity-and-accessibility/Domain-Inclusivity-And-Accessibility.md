# Inclusivity & Accessibility

ADA/AODA compliance, sensory accommodations, universal design, and equity/diversity/inclusion programs for venues. This domain ensures that venues are welcoming, accessible, and equitable for all guests, staff, and community members.

## Scope

### In Scope

ADA and AODA compliance, accessible seating and sightlines, sensory accommodations (quiet rooms, assistive listening), universal design principles, EDI programs and policies, cultural inclusivity, language services and translation, service animal policies, accessible parking and transportation, community accessibility partnerships.

### Out of Scope

Legal ADA compliance tracking (covered in legal-compliance-and-licensing), accessible ticketing allocation (covered in ticketing-and-box-office), guest experience strategy (covered in guest-experience).

## Key Functions

ADA/AODA compliance management, accessible design review, sensory accommodation programs, EDI policy development, cultural inclusivity programming, language services coordination, accessibility training, community partnership management.
