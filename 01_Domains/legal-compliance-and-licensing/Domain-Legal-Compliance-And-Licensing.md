# Legal, Compliance & Licensing

Contracts, intellectual property, permits, liquor licensing, and regulatory compliance that govern venue operations. This domain covers the legal framework within which all other operational domains must operate, including jurisdiction-specific requirements.

## Scope

### In Scope

Contract management and legal review, intellectual property (venue naming, trademarks), permits and licensing (occupancy, liquor, food service, entertainment), regulatory compliance tracking, labor law compliance, privacy and data protection (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA), insurance requirements, ADA/AODA legal compliance, health and safety regulations.

### Out of Scope

Insurance program management (covered in financial-operations/safety-and-risk), HR labor relations (covered in people-and-culture), food safety operations (covered in food-and-beverage).

## Key Functions

Contract drafting and review, permit and license management, regulatory compliance monitoring, privacy and data protection compliance, labor law compliance, liquor licensing management, IP protection, legal risk assessment.
