# Logistics & Warehouse

Receiving, storage, inventory management, distribution, and equipment management within venues. This domain covers the physical movement of goods and equipment from delivery through deployment and the warehouse operations that support event execution.

## Scope

### In Scope

Receiving dock operations, warehouse and storage management, inventory tracking and control, equipment management and tracking, distribution to event spaces, freight and shipping coordination, asset tagging and management, loading dock scheduling, storage optimization.

### Out of Scope

Vendor selection and procurement (covered in supply-chain-and-procurement), event setup labor (covered in event-operations), F&B inventory management (covered in food-and-beverage).

## Key Functions

Receiving and dock management, warehouse operations, inventory control, equipment tracking, internal distribution, freight coordination, asset management, dock scheduling, storage layout optimization.
