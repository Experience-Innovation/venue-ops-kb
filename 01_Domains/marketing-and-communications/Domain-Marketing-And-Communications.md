# Marketing & Communications

Brand management, digital marketing, social media, media relations, and crisis communications for venues. This domain covers how venues communicate with audiences, build brand identity, drive event attendance, and manage reputation.

## Scope

### In Scope

Brand identity and management, digital marketing and social media, media relations and PR, crisis communications, content marketing, email marketing and CRM campaigns, venue website management, event marketing support, community engagement communications, internal communications.

### Out of Scope

Sponsorship sales (covered in commercial-and-revenue), guest feedback collection (covered in guest-experience), ticket marketing and promotions (covered in ticketing-and-box-office).

## Key Functions

Brand management, digital and social media marketing, media relations, crisis communications planning, content creation, email and CRM marketing, website management, event promotion support, community relations communications.
