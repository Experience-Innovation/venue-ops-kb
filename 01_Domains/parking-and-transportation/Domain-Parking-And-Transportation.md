# Parking & Transportation

Parking operations, transit coordination, ride-share management, and traffic management for venues. This domain covers the first and last touchpoints of the guest journey and the revenue and operational complexity of managing vehicle access.

## Scope

### In Scope

Parking lot and garage operations, parking revenue management, transit partnerships and coordination, ride-share staging and management, traffic management plans, VIP and accessible parking, pre-paid parking programs, parking technology (PARCS, LPR), bicycle and alternative transportation, pedestrian access.

### Out of Scope

Crowd flow inside the venue (covered in crowd-management), perimeter security (covered in security), guest wayfinding inside (covered in guest-experience).

## Key Functions

Parking operations management, revenue collection and optimization, traffic plan development and execution, transit coordination, ride-share zone management, accessible parking compliance, parking technology management, event-day traffic control.
