# People & Culture

Workforce management across permanent staff, part-time event workers, contractors, and volunteers. This domain covers recruitment, training, scheduling, retention, organizational culture, labor relations, and the development programs that build venue operational capability.

## Scope

### In Scope

Recruitment and onboarding, training and certification programs, workforce scheduling and optimization, volunteer management, labor relations and collective bargaining, employee engagement and retention, organizational culture development, performance management, succession planning, seasonal staffing models.

### Out of Scope

Labor law compliance (covered in legal-compliance-and-licensing), payroll and compensation budgets (covered in financial-operations), contractor procurement (covered in supply-chain-and-procurement).

## Key Functions

Recruitment and talent acquisition, training program design and delivery, workforce scheduling, volunteer coordination, employee engagement programs, labor relations management, performance evaluation, seasonal staffing ramp-up/down.
