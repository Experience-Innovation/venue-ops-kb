# Premium & VIP

Suites, clubs, hospitality spaces, premium F&B, and VIP services that deliver elevated experiences and disproportionate revenue per guest. This domain covers the design, operation, and service delivery of premium venue products.

## Scope

### In Scope

Suite operations and management, club and lounge operations, premium hospitality programming, VIP services and concierge, premium F&B design and delivery, suite holder relationship management, premium access and credentialing, hospitality staff training, premium space design and renovation.

### Out of Scope

Premium sales and pricing (covered in commercial-and-revenue), general F&B operations (covered in food-and-beverage), general guest experience (covered in guest-experience).

## Key Functions

Suite operations and turnover, club and lounge management, VIP concierge services, premium F&B coordination, suite holder relationship management, premium credentialing, hospitality staff management, premium space maintenance.
