# Premium Suite Operations

Arena and stadium suite operations encompassing pre-event setup from banquet event orders, in-suite F&B service, POS handling, private entrances, concierge support, and post-event turnover for large suite inventories.

## Overview

Arena premium departments operate large inventories of suites and club seats, delivering full F&B service, private entrances, and concierge-level support. Suite attendant operations are more extensive and game-driven than hospitality offerings in most performing arts centres or convention centres, with pre-event setup based on banquet event orders, in-suite service during events, and rapid post-event turnover.

## Key Operations

- Pre-event suite setup based on banquet event orders (BEOs)
- In-suite food and beverage service during events
- Point-of-sale handling for suite-level purchases
- Private entrance management and credential verification
- Concierge services and suite holder relationship management
- Post-event turnover including cleaning, inventory, and restocking

## Staffing Structure

Suite operations teams include suite attendants, lead suite attendants, and premium supervisors who focus on high-touch service, inventory management, and revenue capture. Staffing scales with suite count and event frequency, with arena playdate schedules (80-150+ events per year) demanding high operational cadence.

## Venue Type Variations

Arenas typically operate the most intensive suite programs due to high event density and intimate suite proximity to the action. Stadiums have larger suite inventories but fewer events. Convention centres offer suite-equivalent hospitality in different formats (executive meeting rooms, VIP lounges). Performing arts centres may have a small number of boxes but not at arena/stadium operational scale.
