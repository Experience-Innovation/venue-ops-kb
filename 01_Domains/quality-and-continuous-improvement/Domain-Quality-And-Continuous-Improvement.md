# Quality & Continuous Improvement

CI methodology, lean operations, standards-based management, auditing, and process improvement for venues. This domain covers the frameworks and practices that drive systematic operational improvement, from frontline Kaizen to enterprise-wide quality management systems.

## Scope

### In Scope

Continuous improvement methodology (Lean, Six Sigma, Kaizen), quality management systems (ISO 9001, Baldrige, EFQM), internal auditing and assessment, process mapping and improvement, benchmarking programs, awards and recognition programs, operational standards development, best practice sharing, improvement culture development.

### Out of Scope

Strategic planning (covered in strategy-and-governance), performance metrics and dashboards (covered in data-and-analytics), training program delivery (covered in people-and-culture).

## Key Functions

CI program management, quality management system maintenance, internal audit coordination, process improvement facilitation, benchmarking analysis, awards program participation, operational standards development, improvement culture cultivation.
