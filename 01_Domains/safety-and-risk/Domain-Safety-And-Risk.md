# Safety & Risk

Life safety, fire protection, emergency management, and enterprise risk assessment for venues hosting large public gatherings. This domain encompasses the regulatory frameworks, operational protocols, and preparedness programs that protect lives and manage organizational risk.

## Scope

### In Scope

Life safety systems (fire alarm, sprinkler, emergency lighting), fire protection and prevention, emergency management plans and exercises, emergency evacuation procedures, risk assessment and mitigation, incident command systems, medical services and first aid, weather emergency protocols, business continuity planning, insurance and risk transfer.

### Out of Scope

Physical security operations (covered in security), crowd dynamics and flow (covered in crowd-management), building code compliance for construction (covered in facilities-and-building-systems).

## Key Functions

Emergency management planning, life safety system maintenance, fire protection oversight, risk assessment and mitigation, incident command activation, medical services coordination, emergency drill execution, business continuity management, insurance program management.
