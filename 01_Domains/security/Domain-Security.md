# Security

Physical security, access control, surveillance, and protective services for venues. This domain covers the systems, personnel, and protocols that secure facilities, protect assets, manage access, and respond to security threats including both physical and cyber dimensions.

## Scope

### In Scope

Physical security operations, access control systems and credentialing, CCTV and surveillance, guard force management (in-house and contracted), executive and VIP protection, perimeter security, bag checks and screening, cybersecurity for operational technology, security operations centers, threat assessment, counter-terrorism preparedness.

### Out of Scope

Crowd flow management (covered in crowd-management), emergency evacuation (covered in safety-and-risk), IT cybersecurity for business systems (covered in technology-and-digital).

## Key Functions

Access control and credentialing, surveillance system operation, guard force deployment and management, VIP protection services, screening and search operations, security operations center management, threat and vulnerability assessment, security technology management, incident response.
