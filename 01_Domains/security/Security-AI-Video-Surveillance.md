# AI-Powered Video Surveillance and Analytics

AI-enhanced CCTV and video analytics systems for venues, providing anomaly detection, crowd density visualization, behavioral analytics, and integrated threat response.

## Overview

AI video analytics transforms passive CCTV into an active security tool. Major arenas deploy 300+ cameras with a mix of fixed 4K/30fps and PTZ cameras, increasingly augmented by AI analytics.

## Capabilities

- **Anomaly detection:** Identifying unusual behavior patterns in real-time
- **Crowd density visualization:** Heat maps and occupancy monitoring
- **Behavioral analytics:** Detecting aggressive behavior, abandoned objects, loitering
- **Virtual tripwires:** Alert triggers for perimeter and restricted area breaches
- **24/7 automated alert escalation:** Reducing human monitoring fatigue

## Key Platforms

- **Avigilon Unity (Motorola Solutions):** Integrated AI analytics with access control
- **IntelexVision Sentry:** AI anomaly detection for arenas/stadiums
- **IntelliSee:** Weapon detection, crowd density, perimeter control with RapidSOS 911 integration
- **Hanwha Vision AI:** Stadium surveillance and threat detection
- **Convergint AI analytics:** Video analytics transformation for security operations

## Convergence Trend

Physical and cyber security are converging through SIEM platforms that ingest firewall logs, access control events, and camera metadata into a unified operational picture.
