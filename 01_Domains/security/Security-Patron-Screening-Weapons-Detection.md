# Patron Screening and Weapons Detection

Entry screening technologies and protocols including walk-through metal detectors, AI-based weapons detection, clear bag policies, and accessible screening accommodations.

## Overview

Patron screening at venue entry balances security effectiveness with guest experience and throughput. Technology is shifting from traditional magnetometers to frictionless, AI-based systems.

## Technology Spectrum

- **Traditional walk-through metal detectors:** High detection, slower throughput
- **Next-generation AI weapons detection:** Frictionless screening at walking speed (e.g., Xtract One SmartGateway)
- **AI-assisted X-ray:** For bag screening at high-volume entries
- **Clear bag policies:** Reducing screening time by limiting opaque bags

## Operational Design

- Secondary screening areas for items flagged at primary
- Accessible screening lanes for guests with disabilities, service animals, or medical devices
- Separate screening for VIP/premium entry points
- Staff trained in cultural sensitivity during screening

## Emerging Technology

- Gun detection technology for parking lots and tailgating areas to identify exposed weapons before building entry
- Computer vision systems integrated with CCTV for behavioral pattern recognition
