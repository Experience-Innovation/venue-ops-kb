# Strategy & Governance

Strategic planning, board governance, performance management, and the leadership structures that set direction for venue organizations. This domain covers the top-level decision-making frameworks that guide investment, growth, and operational priorities across all other domains.

## Scope

### In Scope

Strategic planning and master planning, board and executive governance, performance management frameworks, KPI and balanced scorecard, organizational design, stakeholder engagement, public authority governance (for municipal venues), strategic partnerships, industry benchmarking, change leadership.

### Out of Scope

Financial management (covered in financial-operations), quality and CI methodology (covered in quality-and-continuous-improvement), marketing strategy (covered in marketing-and-communications).

## Key Functions

Strategic plan development, board governance support, performance framework management, organizational design, stakeholder engagement, master planning, industry benchmarking, change leadership, strategic partnership development.
