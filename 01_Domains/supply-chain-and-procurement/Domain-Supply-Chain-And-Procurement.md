# Supply Chain & Procurement

Sourcing, vendor management, contract procurement, and purchasing operations that supply venues with the goods and services required for operations. This domain covers the upstream supply relationships and procurement processes that feed every other operational function.

## Scope

### In Scope

Strategic sourcing and vendor selection, RFP/RFQ management, contract procurement and negotiation, purchase order management, vendor performance management, supplier diversity programs, procurement compliance, group purchasing organizations (GPOs), sustainable procurement, vendor onboarding.

### Out of Scope

Warehouse and inventory management (covered in logistics-and-warehouse), F&B menu sourcing specifics (covered in food-and-beverage), financial contract management (covered in financial-operations).

## Key Functions

Vendor sourcing and selection, RFP management, contract negotiation, purchase order processing, vendor performance evaluation, supplier diversity tracking, procurement compliance, GPO relationship management.
