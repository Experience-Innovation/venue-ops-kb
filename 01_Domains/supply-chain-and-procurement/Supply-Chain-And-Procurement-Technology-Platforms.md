# Procurement Technology Platforms

Venue procurement technology spans e-procurement portals, spend analytics platforms, vendor management systems, and GPO-integrated ordering tools that connect purchasing decisions to financial controls and sustainability reporting [[Source-0426-Foodbuy-GPO]].

## GPO Technology Platforms

Major GPOs provide proprietary technology platforms: Foodbuy's platform delivers automated rebate tracking and spend transparency reporting. Avendra's AI-powered Mosaic platform unifies supplier, distributor, and contract data into real-time procurement intelligence. Entegra provides digital tools and advisory services. These platforms reduce manual reconciliation and provide visibility into contract compliance [[Source-0426-Foodbuy-GPO]] [[Source-0427-Entegra-Sodexo-GPO]].

## E-Procurement Integration

Venue e-procurement workflows connect ordering systems to the EMS (Momentus/Ungerboeck) event calendar, enabling event-driven purchasing that automatically scales orders based on confirmed attendance and menu selections. Integration with financial systems (Sage Intacct, Oracle NetSuite) provides real-time COGS tracking against event budgets [[Source-0426-Foodbuy-GPO]].

## Spend Analytics and Compliance Monitoring

Advanced procurement platforms provide dashboards tracking GPO contract compliance, category spend distribution, supplier performance metrics, rebate accruals, and sustainability procurement percentages. Compliance monitoring flags purchases made outside contracted pricing — a common source of cost leakage when operational staff bypass GPO channels for convenience [[Source-0427-Entegra-Sodexo-GPO]].
