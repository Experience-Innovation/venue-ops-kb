# Sustainability & Environmental

Environmental stewardship and sustainability practices for venues, including energy management, waste reduction, water conservation, carbon accounting, green certifications, and circular economy practices. Venues are large energy consumers and waste generators, making this domain operationally significant.

## Scope

### In Scope

Energy management and efficiency, waste reduction and diversion, water conservation, carbon accounting and reporting, green building certifications (LEED, BREEAM, Green Globe), sustainable procurement practices, circular economy initiatives, environmental compliance, climate adaptation, sustainability reporting.

### Out of Scope

Building systems engineering (covered in facilities-and-building-systems), environmental regulations as legal compliance (covered in legal-compliance-and-licensing), sustainable F&B sourcing (covered in food-and-beverage).

## Key Functions

Energy management and monitoring, waste stream management, water conservation programs, carbon footprint calculation and reporting, green certification pursuit and maintenance, sustainability program coordination, environmental compliance monitoring, stakeholder sustainability reporting.
