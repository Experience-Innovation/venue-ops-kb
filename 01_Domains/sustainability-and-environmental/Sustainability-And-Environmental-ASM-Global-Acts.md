# ASM Global Acts Portfolio Sustainability Transformation

ASM Global Acts, launched in 2023, is the largest-scale sustainability transformation program in the venue management industry, spanning a portfolio of 350+ venues worldwide. The program establishes a unified, time-bound commitment structure covering energy, carbon, waste, water, plastic, and supply-chain dimensions. Its scope and specificity set an industry benchmark for how portfolio operators can move from venue-by-venue sustainability pilots to systematic, measurable, enterprise-wide transformation.

## Framework / Model / Program

ASM Global Acts organizes commitments into six quantified pillars with explicit target years: 25% energy reduction by 2030, 20% renewable energy by 2025, carbon neutral by 2050, 50%+ waste diversion by 2025, elimination of single-use plastic by 2025, 25% water reduction by 2030, and 50% reportable spend with environmentally compliant suppliers by 2030. The framework is designed for portfolio roll-up — targets are set at enterprise level but execution is localized to each venue's infrastructure, utility mix, and jurisdictional context. Reporting cadence, supplier scorecard mechanics, and capital planning alignment are built into the operating model so sustainability outcomes compound across the portfolio rather than remaining siloed. The program positions sustainability as a condition of operation, not a discretionary initiative.

## Operational Application in Venues

For individual venues inside the ASM Global portfolio, Acts translates into capital plan adjustments (LED retrofits, HVAC upgrades, renewable procurement contracts), operational SOP changes (waste sorting, water recovery, plastic elimination in F&B), and procurement gate changes (supplier compliance screening). For venues outside the portfolio, ASM Global Acts functions as a structural reference model — independent arenas, convention centres, and performing arts centres can adopt the same six-pillar commitment architecture and scale targets to their own baseline. The program demonstrates that portfolio operators can set aggressive enterprise targets without sacrificing venue-level execution flexibility. It also establishes supplier expectations that propagate across the broader venue supply chain.

## Key Metrics / Outcomes

- Portfolio scope: 350+ venues worldwide
- Energy: 25% reduction by 2030
- Renewables: 20% renewable energy sourcing by 2025
- Carbon: carbon neutral by 2050
- Waste: 50%+ diversion by 2025
- Plastic: elimination of single-use plastic by 2025
- Water: 25% reduction by 2030
- Supply chain: 50% reportable spend with environmentally compliant suppliers by 2030
- Launched: 2023
- Industry position: largest-scale venue management sustainability transformation
