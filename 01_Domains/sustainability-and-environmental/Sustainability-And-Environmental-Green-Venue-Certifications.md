# Green Venue Certifications and Awards

Sustainability certification and recognition programs for venues including LEED O+M (green building), BOMA BEST (Canadian equivalent), and Green Sports Alliance Play to Zero Awards (energy, waste, water, health metrics for sport/entertainment venues).

## Overview

Green venue certifications and sustainability awards provide third-party verification of environmental performance. These programs have become strategic differentiators for venues competing for events with sustainability-conscious organizers, particularly in the convention and sport/entertainment sectors.

## LEED O+M (Operations and Maintenance)

LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design), administered by the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC) and by CaGBC (Canada Green Building Council) in Canada, uses a 100-point system with four certification tiers:

- **Certified** — 40-49 points
- **Silver** — 50-59 points
- **Gold** — 60-79 points
- **Platinum** — 80+ points

The O+M (Operations and Maintenance) rating system is specifically designed for existing buildings, making it the most relevant LEED pathway for operational venues. It evaluates energy efficiency, water conservation, indoor environmental quality, materials management, and transportation access.

Hawaii Convention Center achieved the distinction of being the first public assembly facility to earn LEED v4 O+M Gold certification, demonstrating that large-scale venue operations can meet rigorous green building standards.

## BOMA BEST (Canada)

BOMA BEST (Building Environmental Standards), administered by the Building Owners and Managers Association of Canada, is the Canadian-specific equivalent of LEED for building operations. The program assesses five environmental performance areas:

- **Energy and Carbon** — consumption and emissions management
- **Water** — usage efficiency and conservation
- **Waste** — diversion, recycling, and reduction programs
- **Indoor Environment** — air quality, lighting, and occupant comfort
- **Resources** — materials management and procurement practices

BOMA BEST offers five certification levels, providing a progressive pathway for Canadian venues. The program's Canadian-specific design accounts for climate, regulatory, and market conditions unique to the Canadian built environment.

## Green Sports Alliance Play to Zero Awards

The Green Sports Alliance Play to Zero Awards recognize sport and entertainment venues across specific sustainability achievement categories:

- **Energy Champion** — 100% renewable energy procurement or generation
- **Waste Leader** — 50%+ waste diversion rate
- **Water Replenishment** — demonstrable water stewardship and offset programs
- **Health & Wellness** — indoor environmental quality and occupant health initiatives
- **Verified** — new category for 2026, providing third-party verification of sustainability claims

Allegiant Stadium (Las Vegas) stands as a multi-category winner, demonstrating that large-scale sport/entertainment venues can achieve high-level sustainability performance across multiple dimensions simultaneously.

## Strategic Value for Venues

Green certifications serve three strategic purposes for venue operators: (1) they provide credible, third-party validation of sustainability claims for event organizer RFPs, (2) they create structured improvement pathways that drive operational efficiency gains, and (3) they support ESG reporting requirements for publicly funded or publicly governed venues.
