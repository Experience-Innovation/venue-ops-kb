# LEED and Green Building Certification for Venues

LEED v4.1 is the most widely adopted green building certification for large venues, with additional options including BOMA BEST (Canadian market), Green Globes, WELL Building Standard, and ILFI Zero Carbon, each targeting different sustainability dimensions.

## Certification Landscape

- **LEED v4.1:** Most widely recognized globally — points-based across energy, water, materials, IEQ, and innovation
- **BOMA BEST 4.0:** Dominant in Canadian commercial buildings — energy, water, waste, emissions, indoor environment
- **Green Globes EB:** Online assessment tool — more flexible than LEED for existing buildings
- **WELL Building Standard v2:** Health and wellness focused — air, water, nourishment, light, movement, thermal comfort, sound
- **ILFI Zero Carbon:** Most stringent — requires 100% renewable energy on annual basis

## Venue-Specific Considerations

Large venues face unique LEED challenges: variable occupancy, high peak energy demand, massive water consumption during events, and complex waste streams from F&B operations. Convention centres pursuing LEED often focus on LEED O+M (Operations & Maintenance) rather than new construction credits.
