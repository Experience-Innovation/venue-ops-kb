# Net Zero Carbon Events Initiative

The Net Zero Carbon Events initiative is an industry-wide pledge committing event venues, organizers, and suppliers to halve greenhouse gas emissions by 2030 and reach net zero by 2050, with over 200 signatories including major convention centre operators and industry associations.

## Pledge Framework

Signatories commit to:
1. Measure Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions using a common methodology
2. Halve emissions by 2030 from a baseline year
3. Reach net zero by 2050
4. Publish annual progress reports
5. Collaborate on industry-wide tools and methodologies

## Implementation

The initiative aligns with ISO 20121 (Event Sustainability Management Systems) and the Paris Agreement. Venues implement through energy efficiency, renewable procurement, waste reduction, sustainable transport options, and supply chain engagement.

## Industry Adoption

Major signatories include ASM Global, Informa, Reed Exhibitions, and national convention bureau associations. The pledge creates market pressure for venue operators to demonstrate measurable sustainability progress.
