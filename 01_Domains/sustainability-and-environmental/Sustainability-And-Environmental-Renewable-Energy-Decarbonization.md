# Renewable Energy and Venue Decarbonization

Venue decarbonization strategies include on-site solar and battery storage, power purchase agreements (PPAs), renewable energy certificates (RECs), electrification of heating systems, and refrigerant transition from HFCs to low-GWP alternatives per the Kigali Amendment.

## Decarbonization Pathways

1. **Energy efficiency first:** LED lighting, VFDs on motors, BMS optimization — 20-40% reduction
2. **On-site renewables:** Rooftop/parking solar, stadium canopy PV — 5-15% of load
3. **Off-site procurement:** PPAs, virtual PPAs, community solar — 50-100% renewable electricity
4. **Electrification:** Heat pumps replacing gas boilers, induction cooking in F&B
5. **Refrigerant transition:** Low-GWP alternatives per Kigali Amendment schedule

## Financial Mechanisms

- **Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs):** Long-term contracts for off-site renewable electricity at fixed prices
- **Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs):** Unbundled certificates for renewable energy claims
- **On-bill financing:** Utility programs that fund efficiency upgrades through energy savings

## Industry Commitments

The Net Zero Carbon Events initiative commits signatories to halve emissions by 2030 and reach net zero by 2050. Over 200 organizations have signed, including major convention centre operators.
