# Zero Waste Programs and TRUE Certification

Zero waste programs target 90%+ diversion from landfill through source reduction, reuse, composting, and recycling, with TRUE Zero Waste certification (administered by GBCI) providing the leading third-party verification framework for venues.

## TRUE Certification Framework

TRUE (Total Resource Use and Efficiency) requires:
- 90%+ diversion from landfill, incineration, and environment
- Upstream waste prevention strategies (not just downstream recycling)
- Leadership commitment and staff training
- Annual audits and waste characterization studies

## Venue Waste Streams

- **F&B waste:** 40-60% of total venue waste by weight — composting is critical
- **Event materials:** Booth construction, signage, carpet — exhibitor education reduces waste
- **Single-use plastics:** Straws, cups, utensils — reusable alternatives gaining adoption
- **Cardboard/paper:** Highest recycling rate, easiest diversion stream

## Industry Leadership

Major League Baseball, NBA, and NHL venues are leaders in TRUE certification adoption, driven by Green Sports Alliance membership commitments.
