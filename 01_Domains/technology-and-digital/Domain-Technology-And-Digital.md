# Technology & Digital

IT infrastructure, networking, guest-facing digital platforms, and cybersecurity that underpin modern venue operations. This domain covers the technology backbone that enables every other operational domain to function, from Wi-Fi and network infrastructure to digital signage and mobile apps.

## Scope

### In Scope

IT network infrastructure (wired and wireless), guest Wi-Fi and connectivity, digital signage systems, mobile apps and guest-facing platforms, enterprise software (ERP, CRM), cybersecurity for business systems, IT service management, cloud and data center operations, digital transformation initiatives, IoT device management.

### Out of Scope

AV/production systems (covered in av-and-production), data analytics and BI (covered in data-and-analytics), operational technology cybersecurity (covered in security).

## Key Functions

Network infrastructure management, guest connectivity services, digital signage operations, mobile platform management, enterprise application management, cybersecurity operations, IT service desk, cloud infrastructure, digital transformation project management, IoT ecosystem management.
