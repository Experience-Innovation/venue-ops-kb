# EV Charging Infrastructure at Venues

Large venue parking facilities are deploying EV charging stations to meet growing demand, regulatory requirements, and sustainability goals, with Level 2 (7-19 kW) and DC fast charging (50-350 kW) infrastructure requiring electrical capacity planning, load management, and guest experience integration.

## Charging Levels

- **Level 2 (7-19 kW):** 4-8 hours for full charge — suited for multi-hour events and overnight hotel stays
- **DC Fast Charging (50-350 kW):** 20-45 minutes to 80% — suited for quick-turnaround or premium parking

## Infrastructure Planning

- **Electrical capacity:** Each Level 2 station draws 7-19 kW; DC fast chargers draw 50-350 kW. Large installations require transformer upgrades.
- **Load management:** Smart charging software distributes available power across stations based on demand, time, and grid conditions
- **LEED credits:** EV charging provisions contribute to LEED Sustainable Transportation credits

## Business Models

- **Venue-owned:** Capital investment with revenue from charging fees
- **Third-party network:** ChargePoint, Blink, or Tesla install and operate — revenue share with venue
- **Utility partnership:** Utility funds infrastructure in exchange for demand response participation
