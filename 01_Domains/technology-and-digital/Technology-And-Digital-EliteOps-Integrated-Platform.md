# EliteOps Integrated Venue Operations Platform

EliteOps is 24/7 Software's integrated operations platform that unifies four traditionally siloed venue domains — Safety & Security, Facilities Management, Guest Experience, and Event Operations — into consolidated dashboards with shared data, workflows, and KPIs. The platform is designed to replace the typical venue stack of disconnected point solutions (CMMS, incident management, guest CRM, work order system) with a single operational surface. Tottenham Hotspur Stadium's deployment demonstrates the platform at scale in a modern multi-use venue.

## Framework / Model / Program

EliteOps consolidates venue operations around a unified dashboard architecture where incident records, work orders, guest service cases, and event operations tasks share a common data model and can be cross-referenced in real time. Capabilities include real-time incident response, advanced analytics, real-time work order tracking, task prioritization, and KPI monitoring. The platform's integration model means that a safety incident, a facilities issue, and a guest complaint tied to the same location or event are visible in the same pane of glass, enabling faster coordinated response. Advanced analytics layer on top of the operational data to surface recurring issues, bottlenecks, and performance trends across domains.

## Operational Application in Venues

Venue operators running separate systems for CMMS, incident reporting, guest CRM, and event ops typically struggle with data reconciliation, cross-domain incident correlation, and consolidated reporting to ownership. EliteOps' integration model addresses those pain points by collapsing the stack into one platform, which reduces system-administration overhead, shortens mean time to resolution on cross-domain incidents, and enables portfolio-level KPI dashboards. Stadiums, arenas, convention centres, and performing arts centres with sufficient operational volume benefit most — small venues may be better served by lighter-weight tools. The Tottenham Hotspur Stadium deployment illustrates fit for a flagship multi-use venue with high event frequency and complex operational coordination requirements.
