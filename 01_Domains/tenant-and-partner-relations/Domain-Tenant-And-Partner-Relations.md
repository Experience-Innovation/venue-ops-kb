# Tenant & Partner Relations

Tenant management, partner SLAs, co-tenancy agreements, and the relationship management that governs shared-use venue environments. This domain covers how venues manage the organizations that occupy or regularly use their spaces, from anchor tenants to convention exhibitors.

## Scope

### In Scope

Anchor tenant relationships (sports teams, performing arts companies), co-tenancy agreements, partner SLA management, tenant improvement coordination, shared services agreements, licensee management, exclusive use agreements, tenant communication, move-in/move-out coordination.

### Out of Scope

Event booking for one-time clients (covered in booking-and-sales), sponsorship partnerships (covered in commercial-and-revenue), vendor contracts (covered in supply-chain-and-procurement).

## Key Functions

Tenant relationship management, SLA development and monitoring, co-tenancy agreement administration, tenant improvement oversight, shared services coordination, licensee management, tenant communication, occupancy coordination.
