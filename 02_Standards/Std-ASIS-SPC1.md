# ANSI/ASIS SPC.1-2009 — Organizational Resilience

ASIS International's resilience standard with 22 controls spanning prevention, preparedness, mitigation, response, continuity, and recovery — broader than ISO 22301 (continuity focus) but less globally recognized outside North America. One of three standards accepted under the DHS PS-Prep program. ANAB-accredited certification provides recognized third-party validation for venues demonstrating security and resilience maturity to DHS, law enforcement, or major event owner risk teams.
