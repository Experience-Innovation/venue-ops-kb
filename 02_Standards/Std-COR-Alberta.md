# COR/SECOR — Alberta Certificate of Recognition

Health and safety management system accreditation issued by the Government of Alberta. COR targets employers with 10+ employees; SECOR for 10 or fewer. Audit must achieve minimum 80% overall with 50% per element. Valid for 3 years with maintenance audits. COR triggers WCB PIR discounts: 10% in Year 1, ongoing 5%, up to 20% total. For a venue with $5M payroll at $3.00/$100 WCB rate, estimated annual savings of $15,000-$30,000 ([[Source-0450-Alberta-COR-PIR-Program]]).

Nationally registered by CFCSA but not portable — each province's certifying partner grants recognition independently. Many Alberta general contractors and public-sector procurement bodies require COR as a contract condition, making it effectively mandatory for venues performing construction or engaging contractors.
