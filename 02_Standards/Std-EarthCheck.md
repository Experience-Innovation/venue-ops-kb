# EarthCheck Sustainable Event Management Certification

Scientific benchmarking and certification across 70+ countries. Progressive levels: Benchmarked → Certified → Silver → Gold → Master. Aligned with ISO 20121, meaning organizations pursuing EarthCheck Master are simultaneously demonstrating ISO 20121-aligned practices. The Responsible Meetings & Events standard covers convention centres, resorts, hotels, and event venues with policies, procedures, training, and performance measurement ([[Source-0452-EarthCheck-Sustainable-Events]]).
