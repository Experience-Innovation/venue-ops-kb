# ISO 14001:2015 — Environmental Management Systems

ISO 14001 requires venues to identify significant environmental aspects — energy consumption, water use, waste generation (including event waste streams), refrigerant use, and hazardous materials — and establish measurable objectives to address them. Recognized in over 170 countries, making it the preferred environmental signal for international event bids where LEED recognition may be weaker. Shares HLS with ISO 9001 and 45001, enabling integration into a unified management system.
