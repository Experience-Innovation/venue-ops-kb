# ISO 20121:2024 — Event Sustainability Management Systems

Pioneered by the London 2012 Olympic Games (building on BS 8901), ISO 20121 is now mandatory for all Olympic Organizing Committees. The 2024 revision significantly expanded scope to encompass impact, legacy, human rights, climate action, and accessibility. Paris 2024 was certified two years before the Games — first to implement the 2024 revised version ([[Source-0449-IOC-ISO-20121-Paris-2024]]).

Convention centres pursuing international congress bids increasingly use ISO 20121 as a bid differentiator. EarthCheck's sustainable event management product is explicitly aligned with ISO 20121 ([[Source-0452-EarthCheck-Sustainable-Events]]).
