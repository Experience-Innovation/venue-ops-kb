# ISO 22000:2018 / FSSC 22000 — Food Safety Management Systems

FSSC 22000 Category E (Catering and Food Service) explicitly covers hotels, hospitality, event catering, and food service prepared for on-site consumption — the precise scope of venue concession and catering operations. GFSI-benchmarked alongside SQF (13,000+ certified sites), BRC Global Standard, and IFS. Formalizes HACCP from a required practice into an auditable system with documented scope, objectives, monitoring, and continuous improvement. Increasingly required by large corporate event clients and global F&B operators (Aramark, Sodexo, Levy).
