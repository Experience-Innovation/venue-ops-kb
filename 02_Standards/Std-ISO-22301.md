# ISO 22301:2019 — Business Continuity Management Systems

ISO 22301 specifies requirements for implementing and maintaining a BCMS to protect against disruptions. For venue operators, the lifecycle maps to: Business Impact Analysis (critical resources, acceptable downtime), Continuity Strategy (power failure, pandemic, supplier loss), Incident Response Structure (command/control with decision authority), and Exercise/Testing. Accepted under the US DHS PS-Prep program alongside NFPA 1600 and ASIS SPC.1.
