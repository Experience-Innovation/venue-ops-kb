# ISO 22343-1:2023 — Hostile Vehicle Mitigation Barriers

Supersedes both IWA 14-1 and PAS 68 from September 2023. Uses structured classification: vehicle category / mass / speed / impact angle / penetration distance / debris distance. Key improvements: updated vehicle categories, stricter fail conditions, debris weight limit reduced from 25 kg to 2 kg, mandatory real-time camera documentation. For North American venues, specify to ASTM F2656 for domestic procurement; require ISO 22343-1 for international or European-sourced barriers.
