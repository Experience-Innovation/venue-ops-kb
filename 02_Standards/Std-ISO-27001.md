# ISO 27001:2022 — Information Security Management Systems

Venues present a rich information security attack surface: ticketing platform PII, payment card data, loyalty records, guest Wi-Fi, access control, and surveillance data. ISO 27001 provides the ISMS covering 93 Annex A controls. Recommended integration: use ISO 27001 as the structural "chassis" and embed PCI DSS-specific controls within it — reduces compliance timelines by 1-3 months. Most expensive ISO standard to implement: USD $15,000-$60,000 for small venues to $200,000+ for larger organizations. Increasingly required for cyber insurance eligibility.
