# ISO 31000:2018 — Risk Management Guidelines

ISO 31000 is a framework standard — not certifiable — providing principles, framework, and process for risk management. Three components: Principles (value creation, integration, uncertainty), Framework (leadership, governance, accountability, communication), and Process (scope/context/criteria → risk assessment → risk treatment → monitoring). Adopted directly by Australia/New Zealand as AS/NZS ISO 31000. Conceptually aligned with COSO ERM for organizations under corporate board oversight.
