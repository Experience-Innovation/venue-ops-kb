# ISO 41001:2018 — Facility Management

Newest ISO standard targeting facility management operations. Covers space management, building O&M (HVAC, electrical, plumbing, life safety), equipment lifecycle, and reliability engineering. Explicitly applies to hospitality, leisure, transportation hubs, and mixed-use developments. Adoption early-stage compared to ISO 9001/14001/45001 but differentiating for venues competing on FM quality and for FM service providers demonstrating portfolio-wide capability.
