# ISO 45001:2018 — Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems

ISO 45001 replaced OHSAS 18001 as the international OHS management system standard. Clause 6.1.2.1 mandates ongoing and proactive hazard identification covering routine and non-routine activities — and critically for venues, "the safety of the public, especially if the organization's activities attract crowds or involve public events." Not a substitute for OSHA (US) or provincial OHS compliance — it demonstrates continuous improvement beyond minimum legal requirements. Canadian certified organizations may qualify for WCB premium rebates.
