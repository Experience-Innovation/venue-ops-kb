# ISO 50001:2018 — Energy Management Systems

Aviva Stadium (Dublin) became the first stadium to achieve ISO 50001 certification in 2013. Canadian implementations have documented average 10% cumulative energy performance improvement within 2 years, with up to $2 million in annual savings. For venues with $5-10M annual energy bills, a 10% improvement delivers $500K-$1M in operational savings. Complements ENERGY STAR Portfolio Manager — ENERGY STAR measures performance, ISO 50001 provides the management system.
