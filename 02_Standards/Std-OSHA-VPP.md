# OSHA Voluntary Protection Programs (VPP)

OSHA VPP recognizes employers maintaining injury rates below national BLS averages. Three levels: Star (highest — industry-leading safety), Merit (approaching Star), Demonstration (innovative programs). Application includes rigorous onsite evaluation of management systems, working conditions, and employee interviews. Union support required for represented workplaces. VPP participants are exempt from programmed OSHA inspections — a significant operational advantage. Re-evaluation every 3-5 years. The US functional equivalent to COR in philosophy ([[Source-0451-OSHA-VPP-Program]]).
