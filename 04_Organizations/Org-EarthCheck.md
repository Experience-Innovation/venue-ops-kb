# EarthCheck

World's leading scientific benchmarking and certification group for sustainable travel and events. Adelaide Convention Centre became the first convention centre globally to achieve EarthCheck Master in 2023. EarthCheck's Responsible Meetings & Events standard is aligned with ISO 20121, covering policies, procedures, training, and performance measurement for convention centres, resorts, and event venues.
