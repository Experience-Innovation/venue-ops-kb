# Standards Council of Canada (SCC)

Canada's national accreditation body. Venue operators pursuing ISO certifications in Canada should verify their certification body holds SCC accreditation and IAF MLA membership to ensure global certificate recognition. SCC adopted ISO 41001:2018 as a Canadian standard, applicable to venue facility management operations.
