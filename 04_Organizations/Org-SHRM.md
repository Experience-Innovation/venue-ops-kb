# Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM)

World's largest HR professional society. SHRM-CP/SCP certifications are directly applicable to venue HR directors and people leaders. The 2025 BEAM framework provides five compliance-grounded questions for evaluating DEI programs in light of evolving EEOC guidance. SHRM's BASK defines nine behavioral competencies and 14 functional HR areas — the competency architecture underlying venue HR professional development ([[Source-0436-SHRM-BEAM-Framework-2025]]).
