# FDA Food Code 2022

The FDA Food Code is a model code published by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration that provides a scientifically sound technical and legal basis for regulating the retail and food service industries. The 2022 edition covers food safety principles, HACCP, temperature controls, and employee health standards.
