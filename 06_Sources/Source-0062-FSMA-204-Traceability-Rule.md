# FSMA Section 204 Food Traceability Final Rule

The FSMA Section 204 final rule establishes additional traceability recordkeeping requirements for persons who manufacture, process, pack, or hold foods on the Food Traceability List. Requires tracking Key Data Elements at Critical Tracking Events with a compliance deadline of January 20, 2026.
