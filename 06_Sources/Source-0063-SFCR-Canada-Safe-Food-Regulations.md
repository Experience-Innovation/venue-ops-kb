# Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR)

The Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR) consolidate multiple Canadian food commodity regulations into a single framework. They establish requirements for licensing, preventive controls (including Preventive Control Plans), and traceability across the Canadian food supply chain.
