# SQF Institute — Safe Quality Food Program

The SQF Institute administers the Safe Quality Food certification program, a GFSI-benchmarked food safety and quality management system. SQF certification is widely used by food manufacturers, distributors, and processors as evidence of food safety compliance in supply chain qualification.
