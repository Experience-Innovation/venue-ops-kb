# Entertainment Technician Certification Program (ETCP)

ETCP provides portable certification for entertainment riggers (Arena and Theatre tracks) and electricians. ANSI-accredited under ISO 17024, ETCP certification is the recognized competency standard for rigging personnel in arenas, convention centres, and theatres.
