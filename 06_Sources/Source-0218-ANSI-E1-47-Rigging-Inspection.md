# ANSI E1.47-2017 — Entertainment Rigging System Inspection

ESTA recommended practice promoting proper inspection of entertainment rigging systems including fly systems, counterweight arbors, and overhead rigging in performing arts venues.

## Source Details

- **Publisher:** ESTA (Entertainment Services and Technology Association)
- **Type:** Regulatory document / ANSI standard
- **URL:** https://www.mainstage.com/pdf/ANSI_E1-47_Rig-2014-2003r4-2_secure.pdf
- **Accessed:** 2026-04-04

## Relevance

Codifies inspection requirements for fly systems, counterweight arbors, and overhead rigging specific to performing arts venues. ANSI E1.47 is one of several ESTA/ANSI standards (along with E1.4, E1.64, E1.43) that govern stage rigging safety in PACs.
