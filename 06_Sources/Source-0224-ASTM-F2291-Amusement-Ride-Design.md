# ASTM F2291 — Standard Practice for Design of Amusement Rides and Devices

ASTM F24 committee standard governing the design and safety requirements for amusement rides and devices, part of the core safety framework for theme park operations.

## Source Details

- **Publisher:** ASTM International (F24 Committee)
- **Type:** Regulatory document / industry standard
- **URL:** https://ia800607.us.archive.org/34/items/gov.law.astm.f2291.2006/astm.f2291.2006.pdf
- **Accessed:** 2026-04-04

## Relevance

Core standard for theme park ride safety. ASTM F2291 addresses ride design and safety requirements, while companion standard F770 covers ownership, operation, maintenance, and inspections. Together they form the foundational safety framework for ride-intensive parks.
