# ICC International Plumbing Code — All-Gender Restroom Provisions

ICC Building Safety Journal technical article documenting IPC provisions for all-gender restroom design across code cycles (2000-2024), including Section 403.1.1 Exception 2 (2021) establishing multi-user all-gender facility fixture calculations at 100% occupant load, and 2024 signage and lockable egress door provisions. The IPC is adopted in 37 US states serving approximately 76% of the US population.
