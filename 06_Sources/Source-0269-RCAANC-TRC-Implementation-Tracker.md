# Government of Canada — Delivering on TRC Calls to Action Implementation Tracker

Crown-Indigenous Relations and Northern Affairs Canada (RCAANC-CIRNAC) official tracking dashboard for implementation of the 94 TRC Calls to Action. Reports approximately 85% of calls "completed or well underway" as of October 2025, a characterization disputed by Indigenous organizations including the Assembly of First Nations.
