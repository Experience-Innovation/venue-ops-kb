# Las Vegas / Clark County — Alcohol Licenses and Related Business Licenses

City of Las Vegas and Clark County official guide to alcohol licensing, defining Full Alcohol On-Premise License (covering sports arenas, convention centres, cultural establishments), Individual Access Endorsement for suite booths, and per-bar fee model ($900 semi-annual per additional bar). Nevada administers liquor licensing at the county/city level, not state level.
