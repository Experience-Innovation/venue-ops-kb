# Smart Serve Ontario — Responsible Alcohol Training Program

Official Smart Serve Ontario responsible alcohol training program — sole AGCO Board-approved RBS training for Ontario. Mandatory for all staff involved in sale, service, delivery, or sampling of liquor including stadium security. Online course (~4 hours), 33-question proctored exam (80% pass), 5-year renewal, $44.95 CAD full / $29.95 recertification.
