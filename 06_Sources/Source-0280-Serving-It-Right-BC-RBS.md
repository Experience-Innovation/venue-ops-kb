# Serving It Right — BC Responsible Service Training

Official Serving It Right (SIR) responsible beverage service program administered by Responsible Service BC under LCRB authority. Mandatory for all licensees, managers, and servers in British Columbia before first shift. 5-year renewal, $35-40 CAD + GST exam fee, free course materials. SES (Special Event Server) variant available for events under 500 capacity.
