# TIPS — Training for Intervention ProcedureS Alcohol Certification

Official TIPS (Training for Intervention ProcedureS) responsible alcohol certification program, the dominant private multi-state US program. Voluntary at federal level but mandatory or accepted in approximately 17 states. Tracks include On-Premise, Off-Premise, Concessions, and Gaming. 3-year renewal, ~$35-50 USD.
