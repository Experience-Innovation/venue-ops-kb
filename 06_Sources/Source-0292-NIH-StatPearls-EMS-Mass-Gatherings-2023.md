# NIH StatPearls — EMS Mass Gatherings Chapter

This chapter, authored by Wolin J and Friedman M and updated October 26, 2023 (NCBI Bookshelf ID: NBK597369), is the current NIH-hosted synthesis reference for mass gathering emergency medical services. It consolidates evidence-based staffing ratios, patient presentation rates, and response time benchmarks from multiple primary sources including Sanders 1986 and Locoh-Donou 2013.

The chapter cites the Sanders 1986 paper for the 0.5-2 per 1,000 patient presentation rate benchmark and the Locoh-Donou 2013 paper for the 95.97% mild presentations statistic. It also documents staffing ratio recommendations including physician (1 per 5,000-50,000 attendees), nurse (1 per 2,600-15,000), and EMT (1 per 2,600-65,000). The chapter identifies seven US states with codified mass gathering medical requirements: New York, Pennsylvania, Maryland, Georgia, Iowa, Oregon, and Washington D.C.
