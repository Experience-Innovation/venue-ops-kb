# NAEMSP Position Statement — Mass Gathering Medical Care

This 2015 resource document by Schwartz B, Nafziger S, Milsten A, Luk J, and Yancey A (Prehospital Emergency Care, 2015;19(4):559-68, PMID 26270473) provides the most comprehensive current statement of medical staffing ratios for mass gathering events. Published as the formal resource document accompanying the National Association of EMS Physicians (NAEMSP) Position Statement, it synthesizes decades of evidence on mass gathering medical care planning.

The document builds on the foundational Sanders 1986 benchmarks and incorporates subsequent research to provide updated staffing ratio recommendations. It is cited by the NIH StatPearls chapter and the EMRA 2015 review as the authoritative reference for mass gathering medical resource planning, covering physician, nurse, EMT, and paramedic staffing levels relative to event attendance.
