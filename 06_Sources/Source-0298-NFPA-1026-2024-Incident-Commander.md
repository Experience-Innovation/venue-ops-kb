# NFPA 1026 (2024) — Incident Commander Job Performance Requirements

This Pro Board document provides the NFPA 1026 (2024 Edition) Chapter 4 Incident Commander Job Performance Requirements (JPRs). NFPA 1026, Standard for Incident Management Personnel Professional Qualifications, establishes minimum JPRs for personnel performing roles within an all-hazards incident management system.

Chapter 4 competencies cover incident size-up and command establishment, Incident Action Plan (IAP) development, resource management, communication operations, ICS integration, and demobilization. The 2024 edition added a new requirement for continuing education and professional development for all incident management positions, including Incident Commanders. NFPA 1026 aligns with the NIMS Incident Command System; certification is available through Pro Board or IFSAC-recognized testing organizations. No jurisdiction has been identified that mandates NFPA 1026 compliance specifically for private venue operators; requirements typically come through permit conditions or contractual obligations.
