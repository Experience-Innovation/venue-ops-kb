# IFC Section 901.7 — Fire Protection Systems Out-of-Service

This FDNY Fire Code Chapter 9 document provides the IFC (International Fire Code) Section 901.7 provisions for fire protection system impairments. The IFC approach differs significantly from NFPA 101: while NFPA 101 specifies a 4-hour cumulative threshold before fire watch is required, IFC Section 901.7 in most editions requires fire watch or evacuation immediately when required fire protection systems are out of service, with no time-delay threshold.

The code official is empowered to waive the fire watch requirement where there is no substantial life-safety risk. The document also covers IFC Section 3504.2, which requires fire watch during hot work activities to continue for not less than 30 minutes after the conclusion of hot work. IFC Section 901.7 is adopted by reference in state and local fire codes including New York, California, and New Jersey.
