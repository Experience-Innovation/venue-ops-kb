# NFPA 1126 Proximate Pyrotechnics — 2025 Edition Setback Distances

This American Pyrotechnics Association training program checklist documents the NFPA 1126 (2025 Edition) requirements for proximate pyrotechnics before audiences. The standard specifies minimum audience separation of 15 feet or 2 times the fallout radius (whichever is greater) per Section 8.4.1, concussion mortar setback of minimum 25 feet per Section 8.4.2, no-smoking zone within 25 feet per Section 8.5.5.1, and airbursts over audience at minimum height of 3 times the diameter of effect per Sections 8.2.13(1) and (2).

Note that NFPA 1126 edition numbering changed between the 2020 and 2025 editions: the audience separation requirements were previously at Section 6.4.1 and concussion mortars at Section 6.4.2 in the 2020 edition. Venue operators should confirm which edition is adopted by their local Authority Having Jurisdiction.
