# Taylor Swift Eras Tour Rio Heat Death — Forensic Report (2023)

This CBC News report, sourcing Associated Press wire coverage, documents the Rio de Janeiro Forensic Medical Institute (Instituto Medico-Legal) post-mortem findings for Ana Clara Benevides Machado, age 23, who died on November 17, 2023 at the Taylor Swift Eras Tour concert at Estadio Olimpico Nilton Santos. The forensic report, released December 26-27, 2023, determined the cause of death as heat exhaustion leading to cardiorespiratory arrest, cardiovascular shock, and pulmonary hemorrhage. No pre-existing conditions were identified.

The concert was organized by T4F (Time for Fun), which had banned personal water bottles at the venue. Firefighters reported more than 1,000 persons fainted during the event. A criminal investigation was opened by the Rio Public Prosecutor's Office, and Brazilian consumer protection authorities launched a separate inquiry against T4F. This incident became a landmark case study in mass gathering heat safety, prompting industry-wide reassessment of water access policies at outdoor venues.
