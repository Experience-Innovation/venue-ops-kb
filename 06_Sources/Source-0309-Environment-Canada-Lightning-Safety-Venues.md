# Environment and Climate Change Canada — Lightning Safety at Large Outdoor Venues

This official Government of Canada webpage provides federal guidance on lightning safety specifically for large outdoor venues. The guidance is consistent with the US National Weather Service framework, establishing that "activities do not resume until at least 30 minutes after the last rumble of thunder."

This source is relevant for Canadian venue operators as it represents the official federal position on lightning safety protocols. The 30-minute post-thunder waiting period aligns with the NWS 30-30 Rule and the NWS Toolkit guidance for venues without professional meteorologist support. Canadian venues operating under provincial health and safety regulations can reference this federal guidance to establish defensible lightning safety policies.
