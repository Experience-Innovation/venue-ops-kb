# IAVM Convention Center Performance Reporting Framework

The IAVM Convention Center Performance Reporting Framework is the industry-standard handbook for convention centre operations metrics. It defines the booking pipeline stages (Opportunity, Proposal/Tentative, Definite, Contracted, Event Execution, Settlement, Lost Event, Cancelled Event, Turned Down Event), standardizes space classification categories (Exhibit Hall, Ballroom, Meeting Rooms, Theatre, Other Rentable), and establishes core KPIs including Revenue Per Occupied Square Foot (RevPOS), Occupancy Percentage, and Opportunity Conversion Ratio. The framework also provides guidance on sales activity metrics, lost business reporting with reason codes, pace reporting for revenue forecasting, and the distinction between Event Days, Use Days, and Dark Days.
