# What Is a Performing Arts Presenter? - Cal Performances

Article from Cal Performances in which the Director of Artistic Planning explains the difference between a performing arts presenter (which programs and markets its own season, bearing financial and marketing risk) and a rental facility (which licenses space to third-party producers). This distinction carries significant financial implications for booking operations, as a presenter bears all marketing and financial risk for presentations while a rental venue simply collects rent. The article notes that orchestras and large dance companies are typically booked furthest out, often 1-3 seasons in advance.
