# Artist Rider: The Ultimate Guide to Stage Demands

Guide to artist and production riders as legally binding addenda to performance contracts. Covers the two rider categories: technical riders (stage dimensions, power requirements, PA and lighting specifications, backline equipment, load-in logistics, production schedule) and hospitality riders (catering specifications, dressing room requirements, hotel accommodations, ground transportation, security protocols). Standard lead time for rider delivery is 4-6 weeks before a performance, longer for large festivals and international tours.
