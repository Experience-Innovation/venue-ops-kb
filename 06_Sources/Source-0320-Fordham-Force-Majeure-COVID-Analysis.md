# Reevaluating Force Majeure Clauses in the Aftermath of COVID-19

Academic analysis published in the Fordham University IP Law Journal examining how force majeure doctrine has evolved following the COVID-19 pandemic. Confirms that post-COVID contract drafting standards have significantly changed, with courts and practitioners establishing clearer frameworks for pandemic-related force majeure analysis. The article covers cases where artists demanded deposit returns from festivals for COVID-cancelled shows, exposing ambiguity in pre-pandemic force majeure provisions.
