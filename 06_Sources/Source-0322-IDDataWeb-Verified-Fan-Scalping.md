# Are Verified Fans an Effective Way to Counter Ticket Scalping?

Analysis of Ticketmaster's Verified Fan pre-sale registration program, which screens fans using behavioral and historical data, device fingerprinting, and real-time risk assessment before issuing access codes for pre-sale windows. The system has been credited with significantly reducing bot activity during high-demand pre-sales. The article evaluates the program's effectiveness as an anti-scalping measure and discusses ongoing challenges with ticket fraud prevention.
