# Live Nation, Ticketmaster's Owner, Settles Antitrust Case

New York Times reporting on the March 2026 tentative settlement between Live Nation and the U.S. Department of Justice in the landmark antitrust case. Key terms include changes to how Live Nation makes ticketing deals with venues, caps on exclusive venue contract duration, requirements for venue inventory sharing with competing platforms, and amphitheater divestitures. The settlement represents a structural shift in North American ticketing market dynamics.
