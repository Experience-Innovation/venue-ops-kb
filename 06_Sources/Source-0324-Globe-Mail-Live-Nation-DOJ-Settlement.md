# U.S. Justice Department and Live Nation Reach Settlement

Globe and Mail reporting on the March 2026 settlement terms between the DOJ and Live Nation. Details include a fine of USD 200-280 million paid to 40 participating states, Ticketmaster exclusive venue contracts capped at 4-year maximum duration, venues permitted to allocate ticket inventory to competing platforms, Ticketmaster required to make technology platform portions accessible to rival firms, divestiture of 13+ amphitheaters, and a service fee cap of 15% of ticket face value at Live Nation-operated amphitheaters.
