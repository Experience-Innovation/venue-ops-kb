# No More Fakes: Fighting Ticket Fraud and Scalping at Venues in 2026

Industry article detailing advanced anti-fraud strategies for venue operators. Covers dynamic QR codes (timed barcodes that refresh every 30-60 seconds, rendering screenshots useless), cryptographic offline QR codes for venues with limited connectivity (outdoor festivals, underground venues), and broader fraud prevention technologies. As of 2026, dynamic barcodes are widely adopted by major venues and platforms including Ticketmaster and AXS.
