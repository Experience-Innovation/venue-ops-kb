# NFC Wallet for Ticketing and Venue Access

Technical overview of NFC-enabled ticketing delivered through Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. Covers how NFC "just tap" tickets eliminate physical credential handling at entry points and enable faster gate flow for venue access. The technology is now a standard delivery target for tickets from all major ticketing platforms, complementing dynamic QR codes and RFID wristband systems in modern venue credential architectures.
