# PCI DSS 4.0.1 for Venues - Outsourced POS and Ticketing Accountability

Analysis of how PCI DSS 4.0.1 affects venue operators who rely on outsourced payment processing through ticketing partners (Ticketmaster, Eventbrite) and POS systems (Square, Clover, Toast). Key changes include increased merchant accountability regardless of outsourcing, network segregation requirements for systems affecting the cardholder data environment, mandatory vendor Attestations of Compliance (AOC), multi-factor authentication for remote POS access, and staff training requirements for fraud prevention.
