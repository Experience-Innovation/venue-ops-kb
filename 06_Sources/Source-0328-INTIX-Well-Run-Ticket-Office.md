# 15 Areas of Focus for a Well-Run Ticket Office - INTIX

INTIX (International Ticketing Association) article defining the 15 core focus areas that characterize a well-run ticket office. Communication (both internal and external), customer service orientation, and operational consistency are identified as the foundational elements. The article establishes the box office as the operational nerve centre for ticket sales, patron services, and accessibility compliance across venue types.
