# ADA Requirements: Ticket Sales - ADA.gov

Official U.S. Department of Justice guidance on ADA requirements for ticket sales at public assembly venues. Establishes that accessible seats must be sold during the same hours, through the same methods (phone, online, on-site, third-party vendors), and during the same stages of sale (pre-sales, promotions, general sales, waitlists) as non-accessible seats. Includes dispersion requirements for accessible seating across price levels, obligations to describe accessible seating features, and identifies staff training as a "critical and often overlooked component" of compliance.
