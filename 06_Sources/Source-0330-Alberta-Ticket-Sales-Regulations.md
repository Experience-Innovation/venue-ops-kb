# Ticket Sales Regulations - Alberta Consumer Protection

Alberta's Consumer Protection Act regulations (effective August 1, 2018) governing ticket sales. Key provisions include: prohibition on bot software for purchasing event tickets; requirement for primary sellers to perform due diligence to detect and prevent bot purchases and cancel bot-purchased tickets; secondary seller refund obligations (full refund required if event cancelled, ticket purchased by bot, ticket fails to grant entry, or ticket is counterfeit); civil action rights for businesses and consumers harmed by bot use. Exemptions apply to non-profit organizations reselling donated tickets for fundraising and platforms that only advertise (not facilitate) sales.
