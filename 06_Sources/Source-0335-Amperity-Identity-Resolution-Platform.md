# Amperity AI Identity Resolution for Customer Data Accuracy

Amperity platform documentation describing its multi-patented AI/ML identity resolution capabilities. The platform resolves identities by combining deterministic signals (email, phone) with probabilistic/behavioral similarity, turning fragmented first-party data into unified customer profiles. Deterministic matching alone misses 40%+ of cross-system associations when fans use different contact details across platforms.
