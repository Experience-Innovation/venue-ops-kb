# IAVM VenueDataSource — Venue Operations Benchmarking Platform

IAVM's VenueDataSource platform provides standardized KPI reports for members to compare performance against similar venues. Described as "the world leader for venue operations and financial benchmarking reporting." IAVM Venue Data Reporting Handbooks provide standardized definitions for performance metrics across venue types. In 2025, IAVM launched Venue Sustainalytics in partnership with TSNN and Honeycomb Strategies as the events industry's first sustainability benchmarking platform.
