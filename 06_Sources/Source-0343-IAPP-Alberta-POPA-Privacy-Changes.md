# Major Privacy Changes, New Requirements for Alberta's Public Sector

IAPP reporting on amendments to Alberta's Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (POPA) requiring public bodies to implement privacy management programs by June 11, 2026. Requirements include privacy impact assessments for high-risk processing and breach notification obligations. Relevant to publicly owned venue operators in Alberta including convention centres and municipal arenas.
