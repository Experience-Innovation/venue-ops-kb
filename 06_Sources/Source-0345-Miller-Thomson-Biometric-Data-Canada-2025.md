# Biometric Data in Canada: Navigating New Federal Privacy Commissioner Guidance

Legal analysis from Miller Thomson LLP covering the Office of the Privacy Commissioner's August 2025 guidance on biometric data. Key requirements: biometric data is treated as sensitive personal information under PIPEDA requiring express, informed, voluntary consent; convenience alone is insufficient justification; non-biometric alternatives must be offered; vendor contracts must mandate technical safeguards, limit use to agreed purposes, and include breach notification obligations.
