# NEC Implements Real-Time Occupancy Monitoring — Intel Customer Story

Intel case study on WaitTime AI deployment at the National Exhibition Centre (NEC) in Birmingham, UK, following a 7 million GBP technology infrastructure investment. The system uses RTSP video feeds from Cisco Meraki smart cameras with Intel Xeon Scalable processors running AI workloads. Capabilities include real-time occupancy data, automatic capacity breach alerts, flexible adaptation for different event-space configurations, and crowd analytics reports. WaitTime's Entry/Exit Algorithm provides anonymous (non-biometric) crowd counting, addressing privacy compliance requirements.
