# IAVM Venue Executive Competency Standards

IAVM CVE competency standards document covering venue executive competencies including tenant management operating principles, stakeholder engagement, service provider contract management, and dispute resolution frameworks. Identifies managing service provider contracts (Domain 6.F) as a "High Risk/Very Important" competency requiring 3-4 years of proficiency. Includes Domain 11 on stakeholder engagement and management, with specific competency 11.A on developing tenant management operating principles.
