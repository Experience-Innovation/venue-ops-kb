# Metropolitan Toronto Convention Centre and Ottawa Convention Centre Value-for-Money Audit

Ontario Auditor General December 2023 value-for-money audit of MTCC and OCC Crown agencies, finding 97 events lost since 2018 due to bid prices set too high, an estimated $52 million in missed economic impact, and systemic misalignment between financial profitability optimization and legislated tourism mandates. The OCC outsources most F&B services to Aramark employing 230 staff, while the OCC's own core team numbers approximately 18 full-time staff.
