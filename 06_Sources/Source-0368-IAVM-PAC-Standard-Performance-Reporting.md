# Standard PAC Performance Reporting: An IAVM Handbook

IAVM handbook for performing arts centres providing standardized KPIs including Revenue per Patron, Revenue per Event, and Occupancy Cost per Square Foot, plus classification of events by presenter type (centre presentations, resident companies, non-profit rentals, commercial rentals). Defines a resident company as "most often an arts organization whose rent is either fully or partially subsidized by the PAC."
