# DOJ Non-Prosecution Agreement — Oak View Group 2025

U.S. Department of Justice non-prosecution agreement involving Oak View Group's venue-management division, describing how a fiduciary venue manager entered into an undisclosed ticketing-related incentive arrangement, resulting in a $15 million penalty and compliance obligations. Establishes governance precedent for conflict-of-interest controls, disclosure requirements, and audit rights in public venue management contracts.
