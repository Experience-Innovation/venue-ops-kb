# Dynamic Pricing in Major League Baseball Tickets

Academic paper from Kennesaw State University analyzing dynamic pricing strategies in Major League Baseball ticket sales. The research examines how prices should rise after tickets initially go on sale then potentially decline as event date approaches. Importantly, the study warns that perceptions of price unfairness among fans "potentially impacts additional revenue streams" including season ticket renewals, media viewership, and merchandise purchases, advising organizations to avoid maximizing ticket revenue for exceptionally high-demand events at the risk of triggering negative price fairness perceptions.
