# Out of the Box: A Dynamic Pricing Model for Professional Sports

Purdue University research paper modeling dynamic pricing strategies for professional sports ticket sales. Documents an NFL franchise that adopted full dynamic pricing across its entire stadium and reported a $7 million increase in ticket revenue. The study modeled NFL dynamic pricing and found 53% higher expected revenue per ticket in specific sections compared to the static pricing baseline. These findings provide quantitative evidence for dynamic pricing adoption in large venue operations.
