# Destination Booking Agreement (DBA) - Destinations International

Resource from Destinations International defining the Destination Booking Agreement (DBA) as the standard client agreement document used by CVBs and DMOs to formalize a meeting commitment to a destination. The DBA documents the booking commitment, cancellation liability, and displacement protection for both the destination and the meeting professional. This standardized instrument is central to the citywide convention booking process.
