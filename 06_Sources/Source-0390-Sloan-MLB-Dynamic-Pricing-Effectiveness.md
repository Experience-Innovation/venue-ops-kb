# Evaluating the Effectiveness of Dynamic Pricing Strategies on MLB Revenue

Research paper presented at the MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference evaluating dynamic pricing effectiveness using actual MLB data. Key finding: an optimized dynamic pricing policy could improve revenue by 2.36% compared to fixed pricing, while a poorly calibrated dynamic pricing model could actually reduce revenue by 0.78%. This underscores the critical importance of model quality and calibration in implementing dynamic pricing for venue ticketing operations.
