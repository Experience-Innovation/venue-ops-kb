# McNamara-O'Hara Service Contract Act (SCA)

U.S. Department of Labor guidance on the Service Contract Act, which requires prevailing wages and fringe benefits for service employees on federal contracts. Relevant to venues with federal service contracts for catering, custodial, security, and maintenance operations.
