Cross-industry frontline workforce study providing corroborating evidence for technology adoption gaps in deskless work environments. Key finding: 75% of corporate leaders believe they invest in frontline technology vs. only 39% of frontline workers agreeing — a perception gap directly applicable to venue operations.
