# DOL Fact Sheet #18: FLSA Section 13(a)(3) Seasonal Amusement Exemption

Federal guidance on the complete FLSA exemption from minimum wage and overtime for seasonal amusement/recreational establishments. Critical for venue operators determining which facilities qualify (seasonal amphitheaters, fairgrounds) versus which do not (year-round convention centers, hotels).
