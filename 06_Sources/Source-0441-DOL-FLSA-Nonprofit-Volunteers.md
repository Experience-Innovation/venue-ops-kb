# DOL Fact Sheet #14A: Non-Profit Organizations and the FLSA

Federal guidance on the legal distinction between volunteers and employees under the FLSA. Critical for venues operating volunteer programs — for-profit venues cannot use volunteers, nonprofit employees cannot volunteer in roles similar to their paid duties, and compensation beyond nominal amounts triggers employee classification.
