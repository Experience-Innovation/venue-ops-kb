# NIST: Baldrige and ISO QMS — A Complementary Relationship

Official NIST documentation positioning ISO 9001 and the Baldrige Excellence Framework as complementary, not competing. ISO 9001 establishes the process-focused, auditable management system; Baldrige drives strategic performance across leadership, strategy, customers, workforce, operations, and results.
