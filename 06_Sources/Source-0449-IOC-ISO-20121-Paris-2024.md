# IOC: ISO 20121 Requirement for Olympic Organising Committees

Official IOC documentation establishing that ISO 20121 is mandatory for all Olympic Organizing Committees — not optional. Paris 2024 was certified two years before the Games and was the first to apply the 2024 revised standard.
