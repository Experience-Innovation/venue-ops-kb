# FHWA Managing Travel for Planned Special Events Handbook

Primary federal guidance for venue event traffic management plans. Addresses permanent venue TMPs, permit processes (60-120 day lead times), and seven-component TMP framework: site access/parking, traffic flow, traveler information, incident management, pedestrian access, traffic control, and surveillance.
