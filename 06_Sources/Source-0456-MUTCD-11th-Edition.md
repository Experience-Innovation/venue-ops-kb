# MUTCD 11th Edition — Temporary Traffic Control for Special Events

Federal standard requiring TTC plans for any planned special event impacting traffic. Part 6 Section 6A establishes the operative standard linking MUTCD to event-day operations. Chapter 6O (renumbered from 6I in prior editions) covers traffic incident management during events.
