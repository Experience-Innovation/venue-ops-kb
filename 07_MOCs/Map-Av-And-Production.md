## Cross-Domain Connections

AV & Production is infrastructure-intensive and intersects with multiple domains:

- **Technology foundation:** [[Domain-Technology-And-Digital|Technology & Digital]] — networked audio over IP, IPTV systems ride on venue network infrastructure
- **Facilities integration:** [[Domain-Facilities-And-Building-Systems|Facilities & Building Systems]] — power distribution, structural rigging loads, HVAC acoustic impacts
- **Event execution:** [[Domain-Event-Operations|Event Operations]] — show management and stage management depend on AV systems
- **Safety overlap:** [[Domain-Safety-And-Risk|Safety & Risk]] — rigging safety, fall protection, fly system inspection
- **Accessibility:** [[Domain-Inclusivity-And-Accessibility|Inclusivity & Accessibility]] — assistive listening systems, captioning integration
- **Marketing content:** [[Domain-Marketing-And-Communications|Marketing & Communications]] — digital signage, LED content delivery
