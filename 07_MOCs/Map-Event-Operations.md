# Event Operations — Map of Content

## Purpose

Navigation and discovery layer for the **event-operations** domain. This MOC organizes 12 concept notes across day-of execution, venue changeovers, client services, and specialized event operations — with reading paths for Event Directors, Operations Managers, and GMs overseeing event execution.

## Domain Overview

[[Domain-Event-Operations|Event Operations]] — Day-of execution and the operational choreography that transforms a venue between events. Encompasses show management, changeovers, client services, and the specialized functions that enable events across venue types (sports, theatre, convention, fairground).

**Coverage depth:** Authoritative (12 concepts) · **VEF alignment:** Operational Efficiency + Guest Experience

## Cross-Domain Connections

Event Operations is the orchestrating domain — it coordinates nearly every other operational discipline:

- **AV/Production backbone:** [[Domain-Av-And-Production|AV & Production]] — show/stage management depends on AV infrastructure
- **F&B execution:** [[Domain-Food-And-Beverage|Food & Beverage]] — BEO drives F&B delivery for catered events
- **Crowd dynamics:** [[Domain-Crowd-Management|Crowd Management]] — ingress/egress coordination with event flow
- **Safety integration:** [[Domain-Safety-And-Risk|Safety & Risk]] — incident command overlay for event-day emergencies
- **Financial reconciliation:** [[Domain-Financial-Operations|Financial Operations]] — event settlement feeds financial close
- **Facilities coordination:** [[Domain-Facilities-And-Building-Systems|Facilities & Building Systems]] — HVAC/space management for events
- **Booking handoff:** [[Domain-Booking-And-Sales|Booking & Sales]] — sales → ops transition via BEO
