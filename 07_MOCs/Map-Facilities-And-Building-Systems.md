## Cross-Domain Connections

Facilities sits at the foundation of multiple operational domains:

- **Sustainability overlay:** [[Domain-Sustainability-And-Environmental|Sustainability & Environmental]] — energy systems, LEED certifications, carbon tracking, renewable integration
- **Technology integration:** [[Domain-Technology-And-Digital|Technology & Digital]] — BMS as a networked system, IoT overlays, cybersecurity of OT systems
- **Safety intersection:** [[Domain-Safety-And-Risk|Safety & Risk]] — life safety systems, emergency power, fire protection systems
- **Event operations:** [[Domain-Event-Operations|Event Operations]] — space management, HVAC pre-conditioning, changeover support
- **Security:** [[Domain-Security|Security]] — access control systems integrated with BMS
- **F&B:** [[Domain-Food-And-Beverage|Food & Beverage]] — kitchen facility requirements, grease trap plumbing
- **Parking:** [[Domain-Parking-And-Transportation|Parking & Transportation]] — EV charging infrastructure as a facilities extension
