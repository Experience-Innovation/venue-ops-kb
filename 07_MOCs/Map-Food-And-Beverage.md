# Food & Beverage — Map of Content

## Purpose

Navigation and discovery layer for the **food-and-beverage** domain. This MOC organizes 13 concept notes into operational clusters, surfaces cross-domain dependencies, and offers reading paths calibrated to different practitioner roles (GM, F&B Director, Compliance Manager, Executive Chef).

## Domain Overview

[[Domain-Food-And-Beverage|Food & Beverage]] — All food and beverage operations within venues, representing 30-40% of non-ticket venue revenue. Spans kitchen production, concessions, catering, bars, premium dining, dietary management, food safety compliance, and F&B technology platforms.

## Cross-Domain Connections

F&B is a deeply cross-cutting domain — most concepts carry governed-by or related-to links into adjacent domains:

- **Supply chain dependencies:** [[Domain-Supply-Chain-And-Procurement|Supply Chain & Procurement]] — sourcing, supplier qualification, pouring rights, GPO purchasing
- **Regulatory overlay:** [[Domain-Legal-Compliance-And-Licensing|Legal, Compliance & Licensing]] — liquor licensing, responsible beverage service, FSMA traceability
- **Premium integration:** [[Domain-Premium-And-Vip|Premium & VIP]] — premium F&B within hospitality packages, suite service, liquor licensing for premium areas
- **Guest interface:** [[Domain-Guest-Experience|Guest Experience]] — F&B is a primary guest touchpoint in every venue type
- **People and culture:** [[Domain-People-And-Culture|People & Culture]] — F&B labor relations, culinary certifications, union jurisdictions
- **Logistics:** [[Domain-Logistics-And-Warehouse|Logistics & Warehouse]] — cold chain compliance, receiving, inventory storage
- **Sustainability overlay:** [[Domain-Sustainability-And-Environmental|Sustainability & Environmental]] — waste diversion, local sourcing, packaging
