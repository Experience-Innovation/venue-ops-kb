# Guest Experience — Map of Content

## Purpose

Navigation and discovery layer for the **guest-experience** domain. This MOC organizes 5 concept notes covering queue management, wayfinding, front-of-house services, pre-event zones, and quality rating frameworks — with reading paths for Guest Services Directors and Operations leadership.

## Domain Overview

[[Domain-Guest-Experience|Guest Experience]] — The end-to-end journey of every person who attends an event or visits a venue, from pre-arrival through post-event. Encompasses the touchpoints, service standards, and emotional architecture that define the venue-to-guest relationship.

**Coverage depth:** Minimum viable (5 concepts, 3 short of working depth) · **VEF alignment:** Guest Experience

## Cross-Domain Connections

Guest Experience is the most cross-cutting domain — nearly every operation touches the guest:

- **Crowd Management:** [[Domain-Crowd-Management|Crowd Management]] — flow, density, ingress/egress affect guest experience directly
- **Parking:** [[Domain-Parking-And-Transportation|Parking & Transportation]] — first-touch/last-touch guest experience
- **F&B:** [[Domain-Food-And-Beverage|Food & Beverage]] — primary guest touchpoint within the venue
- **Premium & VIP:** [[Domain-Premium-And-Vip|Premium & VIP]] — premium guest segment
- **Inclusivity:** [[Domain-Inclusivity-And-Accessibility|Inclusivity & Accessibility]] — accessibility is foundational to inclusive guest experience
- **Ticketing:** [[Domain-Ticketing-And-Box-Office|Ticketing & Box Office]] — ticket-to-seat journey
- **Quality & CI:** [[Domain-Quality-And-Continuous-Improvement|Quality & Continuous Improvement]] — Disney SSDG, Ritz-Carlton frameworks feed GX design
