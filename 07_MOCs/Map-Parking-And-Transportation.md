## Cross-Domain Connections

Parking is often the first and last guest touchpoint — it integrates with multiple guest-facing domains:

- **Guest experience:** [[Domain-Guest-Experience|Guest Experience]] — wayfinding, arrival experience, tailgating zones
- **Accessibility:** [[Domain-Inclusivity-And-Accessibility|Inclusivity & Accessibility]] — ADA compliance, accessible seating trip chain
- **Commercial & revenue:** [[Domain-Commercial-And-Revenue|Commercial & Revenue]] — parking as a revenue category
- **Security:** [[Domain-Security|Security]] — hostile vehicle mitigation, access control at parking
- **Facilities:** [[Domain-Facilities-And-Building-Systems|Facilities & Building Systems]] — garage structures, EV charging infrastructure
- **Marketing:** [[Domain-Marketing-And-Communications|Marketing & Communications]] — guest communication about access and transit
