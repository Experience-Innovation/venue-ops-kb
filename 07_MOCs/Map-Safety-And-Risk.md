# Safety & Risk — Map of Content

## Purpose

Navigation and discovery layer for the **safety-and-risk** domain. This MOC organizes 14 concept notes into operational clusters covering life safety systems, emergency planning, mass casualty preparedness, and specialized risk protocols — with reading paths for Emergency Management Directors, Safety Officers, and Operations leadership.

## Domain Overview

[[Domain-Safety-And-Risk|Safety & Risk]] — Life safety, fire protection, emergency management, and enterprise risk assessment for venues hosting large public gatherings. Encompasses the regulatory frameworks, operational protocols, and preparedness programs that protect lives and manage organizational risk.
