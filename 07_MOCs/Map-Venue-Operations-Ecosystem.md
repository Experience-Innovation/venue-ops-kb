# Venue Operations Ecosystem

This is the master Map of Content (MOC) for the Venue Operations Knowledge Base. It provides a navigable entry point to all 26 operational domains that comprise the venue operations ecosystem.

The 26-domain taxonomy was developed through a middle-out classification approach, anchored in five industry-validated frameworks: Baldrige Excellence Framework, EFQM Model, Excellence Canada, AIPC Best Practice, and IAVM Venue Management School curriculum. It was expanded from an initial 17 domains through three critique rounds (Decision #30, locked Session 9).

## How to Navigate This KB

Each of the 26 domains has **two entry points**:

1. **Domain Overview** (`Domain-{slug}`) — Scope definition, key functions, cross-domain references, standards/organizations, and canonical domain framing. Start here to understand *what the domain is*.
2. **Domain MOC** (`Map-{slug}`) — Curated navigation layer: concept index grouped by operational cluster, cross-domain connections with rationale, reading paths for different practitioner roles, and coverage status. Start here to *find specific content fast*.

Below, each row in the Domain Index table links to the Domain Overview. The companion **Domain MOCs Table** that follows links to the navigation MOCs. For any given domain, move fluidly between the two — they are designed to be used together.

## Cross-Domain Relationships

The 26 domains are deeply interdependent. Key relationship clusters include:

- **Safety Cluster:** Safety & Risk + Security + Crowd Management + Facilities
- **Revenue Cluster:** Commercial & Revenue + Ticketing + Premium & VIP + Booking & Sales
- **Supply Cluster:** Supply Chain & Procurement + Logistics & Warehouse + Food & Beverage
- **Technology Cluster:** Technology & Digital + AV & Production + Data & Analytics
- **People Cluster:** People & Culture + Inclusivity & Accessibility + Legal Compliance
- **Guest Journey Cluster:** Guest Experience + Crowd Management + Parking + Ticketing + Premium
