| Note Type | Estimated Yield from Existing Corpus | Approach | Dependencies |

| Technology | 20-30 | Extract from concept `supported_by:` fields + body text platform references. Momentus, VenuetizeAXS, Ticketmaster, etc. | Phase D template architecture |

**Total estimated new notes:** 70-120 from existing corpus, no new DR needed.

**Recommended sequencing:** Standards first (highest yield, most structured), then Organizations (overlap with Standards), then Technology, then People (lowest yield, highest verification cost).

### 4.4 DR Prompt Specifications

All prompts should follow the DR prompt template v1.0 (created S19, validated S20):
- **Platform:** Perplexity Pro Enterprise Max (proven yield across R-06 through R-10)
- **Format:** Structured with sections per sub-topic, inline citations with URLs, 15-30K words target
- **Exclusions:** No CS/BMO Centre content, no vendor marketing claims, no speculative predictions without evidence
- **Source requirements:** Minimum 30 cited URLs per prompt, preference for regulatory documents, trade association publications, and academic papers over vendor documentation

**Estimated yield per prompt:** 5-15 concepts + 15-25 source notes (based on S20 actuals: R-06=11c/25s, R-07=7c/20s, R-08=13c/20s, R-09=7c/15s)
